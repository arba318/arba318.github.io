About

I am a first-year PhD student in the TIGP Artificial Intelligence of Things program, jointly affiliated with Academia Sinica and National Taiwan University. My research background is in applied embedded and AIoT systems, particularly IoT-based monitoring and decision-support systems for smart agriculture and environmental sensing.

Research Interests

Embedded Systems, Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence of Things, Edge Computing, Applied AI, Smart Agriculture, Environmental Sensing, Resource-Constrained AIoT Systems.

Research Direction

My current research interest is in energy-aware embedded intelligence for resource-constrained AIoT systems, with emphasis on sensing, local inference, communication, and deployment under real field constraints.
