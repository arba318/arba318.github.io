About

I am a first year PhD student in the TIGP Artificial Intelligence of Things program, jointly offered by Academia Sinica and National Taiwan University. My research background is in applied embedded and AIoT systems, particularly IoT based monitoring and decision support systems for smart agriculture and environmental sensing.

Research Focus

My current research interest is in energy aware embedded intelligence for resource constrained AIoT systems. I am particularly interested in how microcontroller based field nodes can adaptively schedule sensing, local inference, and communication under energy, memory, latency, and bandwidth limitations.
